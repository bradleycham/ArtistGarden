Inspiration

The artists of Champlain college are among the hardest working, creative, and self destructive people I have ever met. They literally pour their souls into their work and It is both admirable and horrifying. I created this game in their honor.

Method

This project has birthed an inumerable amount of ideas in my mind and I have had so much fun running with all of them and utilizing my unity experience. The main goal of this project was to experiment with "finding the fun" in a simple game idea. Starting from scratch always helps to solidify your knowledge.

Outcome

After 4 short weeks developing this game, I can truly say that I had a lot of fun using my skills to make parody game that has personal signifigance. The architecture is clean, the game runs smoothly, and most importantly, it is quite fun.

A labor of love

I made this project for the joy of creating something for me and my friends.

Practice and professional development are just a plus.
